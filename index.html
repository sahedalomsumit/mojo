We're redefining metal recycling.

Working with metal comes with a downside: waste. As much as 20% of metal is wasted in manufacturing, with swarf ending up on floors, stored in waste containers, or even worse, in landfills. Our technology recycles that waste into profit, all on-site with zero CO2 emissions.
